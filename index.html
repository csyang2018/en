Short Bio

  • I am currently an Associate Professor in School of Artificial Intelligence at Hubei University. I received my B.E. and M.S. degrees from Wuhan University of Technology, China, in 2009 and 2012, respectively. I then obtained my Ph.D. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China, in 2017, supervised by Prof. Hongbo Jiang. Thanks to CSC, I had the opportunity to visit Simon Fraser University in 2013, Canada, supervised by Prof. Jiangchuan Liu. My research interests include networking and machine learning.

Prospective Students
