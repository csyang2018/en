Short Bio

  • Yang Yang received the B.E. and M.S. degrees from Wuhan University of Technology, China, in 2009 and 2012, respectively. He received the Ph.D. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China, in 2017. He is currently an Assistant Professor in School of Computer Science and Information Engineering at Hubei University. His research interests include edge computing, mobile computing and machine learning (especially federated learning).
